Document Type

Aadhar Card

The Aadhaar card is a unique 12-digit identification number issued to all residents of India by the Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI). It serves as proof of identity and address for various services and transactions in India. It includes demographic and biometric information of the individual.

Document Type

Pan Card

The Pan Card is a unique identification number issued by the Income Tax Department of India to individuals and entities. It is primarily used for tax purposes and serves as a proof of identification. The Pan Card contains a 10-character alphanumeric code that is unique to each cardholder.

Document Type

Driving license

The driving license is a legal document that permits an individual to drive a motor vehicle on public roads. It serves as proof that the holder has undergone the necessary training and testing to demonstrate their ability to operate a vehicle safely. Driving licenses are issued by the respective government authorities and typically contain information such as the driver's name, photo, license number, and categories of vehicles they are authorized to drive.

Marriage Certificate

For Rajasthan only. information about driving licence The Marriage Certificate is a legal document that serves as proof of marriage between two individuals. It is essential for legal and official purposes, especially concerning marital rights, benefits, and obligations. Marriage certificates are issued by the relevant government authority and typically include details such as the names of the spouses, date and place of marriage, and signatures of witnesses.

Document Type

Voter ID Card

The Voter ID Card, also known as the Elector's Photo Identity Card (EPIC), is an identification card issued by the Election Commission of India to eligible voters. It serves as proof of identity and eligibility to vote in elections. The Voter ID Card contains personal details of the voter such as name, address, photograph, and a unique identification number. It is an essential document for exercising the right to vote in democratic elections.
